Starts With Soap Week of Giving
August 7-13

About

During Starts With Soap’s first annual Week of Giving, students across the country will organize and host local fundraisers to benefit our mission of providing essential supplies to the nation’s most underfunded schools. The 2016 Week of Giving will take place from Sunday, August 7 to Saturday, August 13 and will feature seven fundraisers taking place in Florida, Indiana, Iowa, Kentucky, Oklahoma, Oregon and Pennsylvania.
