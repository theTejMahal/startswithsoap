Starts With Soap Week of Giving
August 7-13

About

During Starts With Soap’s first annual Week of Giving, students across the country will organize and host local fundraisers to benefit our mission of providing essential supplies to the nation’s most underfunded schools. The 2016 Week of Giving will take place from Sunday, August 7 to Saturday, August 13 and will feature seven fundraisers taking place in Florida, Indiana, Iowa, Kentucky, Oklahoma, Oregon and Pennsylvania.

ITuesday, Aug. 9 - Iowa Cold Stone Creamery Fundraiser

Date: Tuesday, August 9, Time: 5 - 8 pm CST, Location: Cold Stone Creamery (4701 1st Ave SE, Cedar Rapids IA 52402)

Join Regional Executive Sruthi Palaniappan at Cold Stone Creamery and enjoy delicious ice cream while supporting Starts With Soap. Bring your friends and help promote equitable education!

All you need to do is grab some ice cream at Cold Stone from 5 - 8 pm CST on Tuesday, August 9 and 10% of the revenue from your order will directly benefit Starts With Soap—no need to present a flyer; just show up!

Wednesday, Aug. 10 - Florida Zaxby’s Spirit Night

Date: Wednesday, August 10, Time: 3 - 10 pm CST, Location: Zaxby's (1451 Tiger Park Ln, Gulf Breeze, FL 32563)

Head on out to Zaxby’s Gulf Breeze with Starts With Soap Chief Marketing Officer Madison Thompson. Grab some chicken and help out our country’s most impoverished schools!

All you need to do is eat at Zaxby’s from 3 - 10 pm CST on Wednesday, August 10 and 10% of the revenue from your meal will go directly to Starts With Soap—no need to present a flyer; just show up!

Friday, Aug. 12 - Pennsylvania SWSummerFest

Date: Friday, August 12, Time: 4 - 10 pm EST, Location: Wildwood Highlands Family Fun Center (2330 Wildwood Rd, Wildwood, PA 15091)

Come together with kids from around the Pittsburgh area for a fantastic night of mini golf, go carts, arcade, and more! SWS Co-Founder Siona Sharma is hosting this event in conjunction with Wildwood Highlands to support Starts with Soap and celebrate the end of summer right.

Just bring this flyer to Wildwood Highlands on Friday, August 12 and 20% of everything you spend on food, tokens, and attractions will be donated to Starts With Soap! Join the SWSummerFest Facebook event here for continual updates as August 12 approaches.

Saturday, Aug. 13 - Kentucky Blaze Pizza Fundraiser

Date: Saturday, August 13, Time: 7 - 11 pm EST, Location: Blaze Pizza (4600 Shelbyville Rd, Louisville, KY, 40207)

Join Regional Executive Amanda Tu for a night out at Blaze, a build-your-own-pizza restaurant in Louisville, Kentucky. Enjoy delicious wood-fired pies and help support equitable education for all!

Just bring the flyer (link TBD) to Blaze Pizza on Saturday, August 13 and 20% of your order, excluding alcohol sales, will directly benefit Starts With Soap.

Tuesday, Aug. 16: New Jersey Chipotle Fundraiser

Date: Tuesday, August 16, Time: 6 - 9 EST, Location: Menlo Park Mall Chipotle (55 Parsonage Rd, Edison, NJ 08837)

Come down to the Menlo Park Mall’s Chipotle to grab a burrito or bowl and support the non-profit organization Starts with Soap! Your purchase will go towards projects helping underfunded schools across the country.
